StockPath

This college year, myself along with 4 of my good friends and course peers, have founded a startup called StockPath. The business idea is simple; small businesses especially in the retail space often have trouble with inventory management. This leads to issues in under stocking, overstocking and overall profitability of the business. Tools on the market for inventory management are generally complex, expensive and aimed at larger businesses with multiple channels. This is where Stockpath comes in: the platform is simple to use, offers convenience through mobile stocktakes and tracking, and is affordable.
